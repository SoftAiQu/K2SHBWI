# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone feels safe and respected. This Code of Conduct applies to all participants in the K2SHBWI project, including contributors, maintainers, and community members.

## Expected Behavior

### Be Respectful
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be open to different perspectives and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Respect decisions made by the community

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions focused and on-topic
- Avoid personal attacks or insults
- Don't engage in harassment or bullying
- Report concerns promptly

### Be Helpful
- Help newcomers get started
- Answer questions patiently
- Share knowledge generously
- Provide constructive feedback

### Be Honest
- Take responsibility for mistakes
- Give credit where due
- Be transparent about limitations
- Communicate openly

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- **Harassment** - Any form of unwelcome behavior
- **Discrimination** - Based on identity, background, or characteristics
- **Abuse** - Verbal, physical, or any other form
- **Hate Speech** - Racist, sexist, homophobic, transphobic, or similar content
- **Threats** - Including threats of violence or harm
- **Unwanted Contact** - Persistent contact after request to stop
- **Spam** - Unsolicited promotion or repetitive content
- **Doxing** - Sharing private information without consent

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Report to maintainers** - Email: conduct@k2shbwi.org
2. **Provide details** - Incident description, involved parties, context
3. **Stay safe** - Prioritize your safety
4. **Confidentiality** - Reports are handled confidentially

### Report Format
```
Subject: Code of Conduct Violation Report

Date of incident: [date]
Location: [GitHub issue/PR/discussion, etc.]
Parties involved: [usernames, if applicable]

Description:
[Detailed description of what happened]

Impact:
[How this affected you or others]

Witnesses:
[Any witnesses, optional]
```

Send to: conduct@k2shbwi.org

## Response & Enforcement

Upon receiving a report, maintainers will:

1. **Acknowledge** - Confirm receipt within 24 hours
2. **Investigate** - Gather information and context
3. **Communicate** - Keep both parties informed (where possible)
4. **Take Action** - Implement appropriate response

### Possible Responses
- Discussion and education
- Warning to violator
- Temporary ban from project
- Permanent removal from project
- Report to platform/authorities if needed

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and comments
- Project communications
- Events and meetings
- Related online and offline spaces
